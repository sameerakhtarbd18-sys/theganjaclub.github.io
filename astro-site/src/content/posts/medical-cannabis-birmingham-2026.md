## The legal reality

Medical cannabis was rescheduled on 1 November 2018 under the Misuse of Drugs Regulations 2001. GMC-registered specialists can prescribe it UK-wide. Birmingham has no local restrictions or advantages — the framework is national. Your Birmingham postcode does not affect your eligibility or access.

## Birmingham-specific notes

**Delivery:** Standard next-day courier to all Birmingham postcodes. No local pharmacies currently dispense cannabis-based medicines — everything is specialist mail-order.

**Employment:** Birmingham's economy spans manufacturing, services, and public sector. NHS Trusts follow national guidance. Your clinic can provide a treatment letter.

**Transport:** The DVLA advises against driving if impaired by any prescribed medicine. Birmingham's bus and Midland Metro network provides alternatives.
